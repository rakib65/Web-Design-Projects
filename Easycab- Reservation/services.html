Services

We are specialized in Private Transportation for with our own fleet for individual travelers and groups.

  • Private Transfers

    Transfer between two points chosen by the client for his party only. The client won’t wait for any other client and no additional stops except.

  • Shuttle Transfers

    This is a Shared service with other clients that offers a transfer with one or more stops between the Hotel chosen and the Airport or vice versa. The vehicle use is accordantly with the clients sharing the Shuttle.

  • Speedy Shuttle Transfer

    Shuttle service with other clients that offers a transfer with one or more stops between the Hotel chosen and the Airport or vice versa. The vehicle use is accordantly with the clients sharing the Shuttle. The clients will not have more than 4 stops before their drop off upon arrival or 4 stops after their pick up upon departure.

  • Regular Tour

    Shared service with other clients that offers a tour with specific routes and stops. The tour can be provided on a taxi, coach or similar.

  • Private Tour

    This Service provides a private tour for the client’s party only. As soon as the clients meet the driver they will depart for the tour. The client may choose the route, the stops within the locations and hours booked.

  • Deluxe Transfers

    This Service provides a transfer on a luxuriant vehicle between two points chosen by the client for his party only. The client won’t wait for any other client and no additional stops except.
